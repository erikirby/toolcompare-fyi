Fireflies.ai vs Typeform: Which Is Better?

Choosing between Fireflies.ai and Typeform? Here's a side-by-side comparison to help you decide.

Quick verdict: Choose Fireflies.ai if you need remote-teams or sales-teams. Choose Typeform if you prioritize marketers or product-teams.

Fireflies.ai

PricingFree / $10/mo Pro / $19/mo Business
Rating★★★★ 4.2/5

Key Features

  • Automatic meeting recording and transcription across Zoom, Meet, Teams
  • AI-powered meeting summaries with key decisions and action items
  • Searchable meeting history across your entire team
  • Soundbites for clipping and sharing specific moments from recordings
  • Conversation intelligence for sales teams and deal analysis
  • Integrations with Notion, Slack, Asana, Salesforce, and HubSpot

Pros

  • Auto-joins meetings and records them without anyone needing to remember or click anything
  • Transcription accuracy is good for clean audio, though it struggles with heavy accents or overlapping speech
  • AI summaries capture key decisions, action items, and questions in a digestible format
  • Search works across your entire meeting history — type 'budget decision' and find the exact conversation
  • Integrates with Slack, Notion, Salesforce, and Asana for pushing notes into your workflow automatically

Cons

  • Free tier is limited to 3 meetings total — not enough for a proper evaluation
  • Transcription accuracy varies significantly with background noise, speaker overlap, or accents
  • Privacy concerns about having an AI bot in every meeting can unsettle team members or clients
  • Action item detection is useful but not reliable enough to trust completely; you'll still need to verify
  • The web app can be slow with large meeting histories; searching across 500+ recordings lags

Which Should You Choose?

Both Fireflies.ai and Typeform are excellent tools in their category. The right choice depends on your specific needs:

  • Choose Fireflies.ai if: Fireflies is the AI meeting assistant that joins your calls, records everything, transcribes the conversation, and generates summaries. It works with Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, and Webex — and it's gotten good enough that you'll stop taking notes entirely. The searchable history is the killer feature: you can find any decision, commitment, or name mentioned across hundreds of past meetings.
  • Choose Typeform if: Typeform is the form builder that made one-question-at-a-time forms popular. It's the gold standard for surveys, quizzes, and any form where user experience matters — because let's be honest, most forms are terrible. Typeform makes filling out a form feel like having a conversation. The trade-off is cost: you pay a premium for that experience.
